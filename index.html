Coins XRPL Explained Trustlines & Airdrops




The XRPL is a decentralized, immutable, censorship-resistant, distributed-ledger and new assets can be added using the contract address with a token code. A small quantity of XRP is used to create to fund the reserve & new assets can also receive airdrops.
